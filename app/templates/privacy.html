Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09/04/2026

This is "pasted.sh" ("we", "us", "our"). We are dedicated to protecting and respecting your privacy when using the features this website provides ("service").

Any questions or concerns about your personal information can be emailed to je@pasted.sh.

What information do we hold about you:

We hold data that is automatically collected by Nginx when you make a request to our server, this includes your:

  • IP address
  • User agent
  • Referrer
  • Browser identification (via browserIdent cookie)

This data is collected in the following cases:

  • You browse any page of our service.
  • You upload content to our service.

The data we hold about you is used for identification in the event of violations in the ToS, as well as debugging.

How we use your data:

We use the information we hold about you allow for the blocking of web scrapers, as well as for banning users that repeatedly violate the ToS.

How do we keep your data secure:

We keep your uploaded information secure by ensuring all files are encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Data inside of our database is not encrypted at rest, the only data that may be accessible in the event of a leak is your IP address.

Your rights:

You have a right to request a copy of your data, or to have all of your data that we hold removed, at any time, no questions asked. If this is the case, email je@pasted.sh.

Changes to this policy:

We reserve the right to change this policy at any time, with a 48 hour notice period before any new changes come into effect.

Breaches of our ToS in relation to CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material) invalidate any rights you have under this privacy policy. The content in question will be removed and reported to NCMEC, with relevant information passed onto the appropriate authorities, and your network will be blocked from accessing pasted.sh
